How it works

MisterLocation is the easiest way to list, discover and book studios and locations. Find the best studios and locations, get in touch with the manager, and seamlessly pay with a few clicks. We take care of the rest so you can focus on your shoot or event!


If you want to book a studio/location

Booking studios and locations has never been easier! It's easy (and FREE!) to get started and as soon as you create a profile you can request, book, and pay. Here are a few tips:

  1. Be detailed in your profile. The studios/locations want to know who will make use of their service before they accept a booking.
  2. Book directly or get in touch first. If you have any special requests, questions regarding equipment, or anything else you can contact studios/locations directly from their listing. Please keep in mind we make the process easier for studios/locations so they prefer to receive bookings through our platform.
  3. Share! Our goal is to connect artists, producers and event bookers with the best studios and locations, so help us build our community! When you post on social media, use #MisterLocation and we will reshare and offer occasional discounts to those that help spread the word.


If you are a studio/location manager

We make it easier for you to promote your studio/location and manage your bookings! We work with artists, producers and event bookers that are always looking for new places. Here are a few tips when using MisterLocation:

  1. Be detailed in your profile and listing. Please provide a compelling description, equipment, and also any past notable clients. We want to help market your studio/location to our community as best as possible. Also, please add professional quality pictures!
  2. Identify your schedule on your listing. This way (potential) bookers can see directly when your studio/location is available and pay you as quickly as possible.
  3. Share! Our goal is to connect artists, producers and event bookers with your studio so help us spread the word and build our community! When you post on social media on your studio/location, use #MisterLocation and we will reshare and offer occasional discounts to those that help spread the word.


If you need help getting started or at any time please reach out to us at support@misterlocation.com.


FAQ – If you want to book a studio/location

Bookings


How do I change or cancel my booking? 

To do so, you will have to contact the manager of the listing you booked. It is up to him/her to decide if a refund will be given. 


Account


How can I manage my email addresses?

You can set multiple email addresses for your user account. 

How to add a secondary email address

  • Log in to MisterLocation.
  • Open your user settings panel ("Settings" from the dropdown under your photo/name).
  • Open the "Account" tab.
  • In the "Email addresses" section, click on "+ Add new email address".
  • Add your second email address. Check "Receive notifications" if you want to receive notifications at this address.
  • Click "Save".
  • Check your email inbox for instructions on how to confirm the address.
  • Follow the instructions. You will see the message "The email you entered is now confirmed.".

That's it! Your secondary email address is now set and confirmed. 


How to remove a secondary email address

  • Log in to the MisterLocation.
  • Open your user settings panel ("Settings" from the dropdown under your photo/name).
  • Open the "Account" tab.
  • In the "Email addresses" section, remove the address by clicking the "X".
  • Confirm that you want to delete the secondary email address.
  • Click "Save".

Your secondary email address is now removed.


How to change a primary email address

  • Create at least one secondary email (see instructions above).
  • Make sure that the "new" primary email address can receive notifications (check the box under "Receive notifications" for this address).
  • Remove the email addresses that are no longer wanted (see instructions above).

That's it! Your primary email address has now been changed.


How can I delete my account and personal information?

You can delete your account from your personal settings:

  • Log in to MisterLocation.
  • From the top bar, click on profile picture and choose Settings.
  • Open “Settings” and switch to the “Account” tab.
  • Follow the instructions and click “Permanently delete my account”.

What information is deleted when I remove my account? 

When you decide to delete your account, all of your personal information (such as name, phone number, address, email, profile picture, payout details etc.) are removed. Interactions with other users (such as conversations with other people, transactions, reviews given to others, comments, etc.) are not deleted. However, the username will no longer be displayed next to this content.

Is it possible to delete a user account at any time? 

A user can't delete their account if they have pending transactions. As soon as all transactions are completed, the user account can be deleted.

Is it possible to recover a deleted user account? 

Recovering a deleted user account is not possible. Delete your account with caution!



Payment methods


How do I pay for my booking?

You can either pay with their credit card or using their PayPal account.

What credit cards are supported?

The most common credit cards are accepted. On this page you can check what cards are allowed in your country.

How does the PayPal payment flow work?

The following steps occur in a paid transaction:

  • The seller posts a listing and defines a price (per hour or per day) for the service.
  • The buyer opens the listing page and clicks on “Book”. The buyer needs to choose the start and end date or number of hours of the booking  before clicking “Book”.  
  • On the following page, the buyer reviews the details of the purchase and clicks "Checkout with PayPal". They are taken to PayPal to complete the payment.
  • Once the payment is done, the buyer is taken to a page that displays the transaction details. They are told that the seller needs to accept the transaction before the money is transferred.
  • The seller gets an email notification of a new transaction that needs to be accepted. If the transaction is not accepted within 3 days, the transaction is canceled and no money is transferred. Once accepted, the money (minus PayPal’s payment processing fee) is transferred to the seller’s PayPal account. Both parties get a receipt of the transaction via email.
  • Both users can then discuss freely to agree on details. When the order is completed (the service has been provided), they can mark the order as completed.
  • Users can then review each other


If you are paying with your credit card, PayPal may advise you to create an account. In some countries or in some situations, this is mandatory. In some other situations, buyers will have a possibility to decline the PayPal account creation. You can contact PayPal support to learn more about the way this works in your country.

Benefits from PayPal protection: If you use PayPal you are eligible for PayPal protection in case something goes wrong. See https://www.paypal.com/us/webapps/mpp/paypal-safety-and-security for more information.





FAQ – If you are a studio/location manager

Listings


How do I create a listing?

Creating a listing requires two steps. First, create a personal profile. With that profile, you can set up as many listings as you want on MisterLocation. 

What if there aren’t any listings yet in my area?

Start the trend! By being the first to list in your area, all of the attention from those searching for a studio/location in your area is directed at you and only you.

How can I provide listings that depend on availability? 

There are two ways in which you can provide listings that depend on availability: 

  • Conversation between booker and listing manager: When a booker asks for a service, they can add an optional message. This is a great place to ask more details about availability on certain hours. The listing manager can then accept or decline the request based on availability.
  • Use the listing description: As a listing manager you can share availability times in the listing description. This information needs to be updated whenever the availability changes.

Payment methods and refunds


Do I need a PayPal account?

A Business or Premier PayPal account is required if a you want to receive money from bookings. If you don't have a Business or a Premier account, you can easily upgrade your existing PayPal account or create a new account. This is free of charge. To upgrade to a PayPal Business or Premier account, go to http://www.paypal.com/upgrade.

Note that in some countries, PayPal might requires some extra verification: don't hesitate to contact PayPal support to learn more about the details related to such an upgrade in your local area. Finally, your PayPal account must be configured to accept payment in your marketplace currency.

Benefits from PayPal protection: If you use PayPal you are eligible for PayPal protection in case something goes wrong. See https://www.paypal.com/us/webapps/mpp/paypal-safety-and-security for more information.
